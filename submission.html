MANUSCRIPT SUBMISSION

Authors must submit their papers to conference Email iceai.contact@sru.edu.in on or before the submission deadline mentioned in the conference homepage.

While submitting the research article, authors should ensure that the research work is novel and has not previously been published, as well as that it is not currently being considered for publication in any other conferences/journals.
The following are the instructions and guidelines to submit your manuscript to ICEAI 2024

Formatting and Paper Template

  • The manuscript file should start with a title that shows author affiliations and contact information, identifying the corresponding author with a special symbol.
  • The following structure will be suitable in many cases:
    • Acknowledgements (optional)
    • Author contributions (if paper has more than 6 authors)
  • There can be a maximum of 4 authors in a paper. If exceeded, the individual contribution made by each author should be mentioned. Do not use academic titles such as Dr., Professor, HoD, Director, etc. in your final paper.
  • Follow the single-column format and the content should fit in the A4 Size.

Abstract Submission Template

Papers must be formatted using the conference paper template. Paper must be submitted in English language.

Review Process

ICEAI 2024 will follow a peer-review process. All submitted papers will be reviewed based on quality, presentation, and relevance to the conference theme. Papers that do not conform to the submission policies will be rejected without review. The most frequent cause of rejections of submitted papers is a lack of new results and plagiarized content. Duplicated submissions to concurrent conferences must be avoided.

  • The conference chair examines all peer reviewer input before making an informed choice to accept or reject the submission.
  • This process takes 2-3 weeks after initial submission.
  • The decision of acceptance/rejection/revision will be communicated via Email to the corresponding author.

Note: Authors should register only when their work has been accepted.
